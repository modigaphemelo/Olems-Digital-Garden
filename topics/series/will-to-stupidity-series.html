The Complete Will to Stupidity Series

Exploring strategic folly and the architecture of character

This series explores the paradoxical concept of "strategic stupidity" - the intentional adoption of seemingly foolish positions or actions that ultimately serve higher strategic purposes. Through five interconnected essays, we examine how what appears as folly from one perspective can reveal profound wisdom from another.
