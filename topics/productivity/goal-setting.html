Goal Setting (OKRs, SMART Goals)

Framework for setting and achieving meaningful objectives

SMART Goals Framework

  • Specific
    Clear and well-defined objectives
    Answers the 5 Ws: What, Why, Who, Where, Which
  • Measurable
    Track progress and measure outcomes
    Quantifiable metrics and milestones
  • Achievable
    Realistic and attainable goals
    Within available resources and capabilities
  • Relevant
    Aligns with broader objectives
    Matters to personal or organizational goals
  • Time-bound
    Clear deadlines and timeframes
    Creates urgency and focus

    • OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)

  • Objectives
    Qualitative, inspirational goals
    What you want to accomplish
  • Key Results
    Quantifiable measures of success
    How you'll measure achieving the objective
